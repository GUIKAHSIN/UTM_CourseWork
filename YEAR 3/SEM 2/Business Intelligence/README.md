## 🌱 Reflection

Business Intelligence provided valuable experience in transforming raw data into actionable insights. Using Alteryx for data cleaning, preparation, and workflow automation demonstrated how data can be processed efficiently before analysis. The course also introduced Power BI for dashboard development and visualization, allowing business information to be communicated effectively through interactive reports.

The projects strengthened analytical thinking, data visualization skills, and understanding of how organizations utilize data-driven insights to support strategic and operational decision-making.
