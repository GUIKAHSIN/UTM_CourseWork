# 🏢 SECP3623 Enterprise Systems Design And Modelling (WBL)

## 📚 Overview

Enterprise Systems Design And Modelling focuses on analysing business requirements, modelling enterprise processes, and designing large-scale information systems that support organizational operations. The course emphasizes system analysis, enterprise architecture, business process modelling, requirements engineering, and solution design before system implementation.

---

## 🌍 Industry Exposure

The course also included an industry visit to Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM), which provided valuable exposure to enterprise technologies and digital transformation initiatives implemented within higher education institutions.

In addition, SAP enterprise solutions were introduced to demonstrate how large organizations integrate business processes, data, and resources through enterprise systems. This provided a broader understanding of how enterprise applications support decision-making and operational efficiency.

## 🚀 Featured Project: Subject Registration & Academic Advising System

The Subject Registration & Academic Advising System was developed as a case study to improve the efficiency of academic management processes within a university environment. The system was designed to digitalize course registration, academic advising, timetable management, and course administration activities.

### Project Objectives

- Streamline the subject registration process
- Improve academic advising efficiency
- Reduce registration conflicts and prerequisite violations
- Centralize course and student information
- Enhance administrative management and monitoring

### Key Functionalities

#### 👨‍🎓 Student Module

- Course registration and enrollment
- Timetable viewing
- Academic progress monitoring
- Add and drop course requests
- Prerequisite validation

#### 👩‍🏫 Academic Advisor Module

- Student advising management
- Academic performance monitoring
- Registration approval workflows
- Course recommendation support

#### 👨‍💼 Administrator Module

- Course management
- Programme management
- Student records management
- Timetable administration
- Registration period configuration

### System Analysis & Design Activities

Throughout the project, various enterprise system modelling and analysis techniques were applied:

- Requirements gathering and analysis
- Stakeholder identification
- Business process analysis
- Use Case Diagram development
- Activity Diagram modelling
- Sequence Diagram design
- Class Diagram design
- Functional requirement specification
- Non-functional requirement specification
- User interface prototyping
- System architecture design

## 🌱 Reflection

Enterprise Systems Design And Modelling provided valuable experience in analysing organizational requirements and translating them into enterprise-level system designs. Unlike traditional programming-focused courses, the emphasis was placed on understanding business processes, stakeholder needs, and system architecture before implementation begins.

The Subject Registration & Academic Advising System project demonstrated how complex organizational problems can be addressed through structured analysis, modelling, and design methodologies. Developing various system models and documentation strengthened analytical thinking, communication, and problem-solving skills while highlighting the importance of proper planning in large-scale software development projects.

The exposure to SAP solutions and the industry visit further broadened my understanding of how enterprise systems support real-world organizational operations. Overall, the course provided a strong foundation in enterprise system analysis and modelling that complements both software engineering and data engineering disciplines.
