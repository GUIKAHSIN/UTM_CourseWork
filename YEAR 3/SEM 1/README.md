# 📖 Course Highlights

## 🤖 Artificial Intelligence

This course introduced the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence, including intelligent agents, search algorithms, machine learning concepts, and neural networks.

### Reflection

Artificial Intelligence was an enjoyable course that provided a broad introduction to how intelligent systems work. The course introduced concepts such as machine learning, neural networks, classification models, and decision-making algorithms, allowing me to gain a basic understanding of AI technologies. Developing ANN and CNN models further strengthened my understanding of how artificial intelligence can be applied to solve real-world problems and support data-driven solutions.

---

## 🗄️ Database Programming

This course focused on advanced database concepts, database programming techniques, and database application development.

### Reflection

Database Programming expanded upon the database knowledge gained from previous courses by introducing more practical database operations and management techniques. Working with MongoDB, database queries, indexing, aggregation pipelines, and database applications provided hands-on experience in data manipulation and management. The course strengthened my understanding of backend data management and reinforced the importance of efficient database design and operations in modern applications.

---

## 🏢 Enterprise System Development & Modelling

This course introduced enterprise systems, business process modelling, and enterprise software solutions used in organizations.

### Reflection

Enterprise System Development & Modelling provided valuable exposure to enterprise-level software systems and business processes. One of the highlights of the course was the industry visit to Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM), which provided insight into how enterprise technologies are applied in real-world environments. The course also introduced SAP solutions and enterprise modelling concepts, helping me understand how organizations integrate business processes, people, and technology through enterprise systems.
