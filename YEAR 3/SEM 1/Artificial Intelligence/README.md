# 🤖 SECI3143 Artificial Intelligence

## 📚 Overview

Artificial Intelligence introduces the fundamental concepts of intelligent systems, problem solving, search algorithms, machine learning, and neural networks. The course explores how computers can simulate human reasoning and decision-making processes.

## 📝 Evidence

- AI Assignment 1
- AI Assignment 2 (SimuLearn)
- AI Assignment 3
- AI Project Proposal
- SimuLearn Proof of Concept (POC)

## 🌱 Reflection

Artificial Intelligence was an enjoyable course that provided a broad introduction to AI concepts and technologies. Through assignments and the SimuLearn project, I gained exposure to intelligent systems, machine learning concepts, and AI-driven solutions. The course expanded my understanding of how AI can be applied to automate decision-making and solve real-world problems. It also sparked greater interest in machine learning and advanced analytics, which are closely related to the Data Engineering field.
