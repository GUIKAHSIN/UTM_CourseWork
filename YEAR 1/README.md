# 📝 Reflection

Year 1 served as the foundation of the Computer Science (Data Engineering) programme by introducing essential computing concepts and problem-solving approaches. Courses such as Programming Technique, Digital Logic, Discrete Structure, and Computer Organization & Architecture strengthened logical thinking and technical understanding of how software interacts with hardware systems.

Programming assignments provided valuable experience in developing structured solutions and debugging code, while mathematics-related subjects improved analytical reasoning and computational thinking. System Analysis and Design introduced the importance of understanding user requirements, documentation, and systematic development processes.

In addition to technical knowledge, Year 1 enhanced communication, teamwork, and time management skills through presentations, discussions, and group-based assignments. These experiences prepared the groundwork for more advanced software engineering, database, analytics, and data engineering subjects in subsequent years.

Overall, Year 1 established a strong academic and technical foundation that supports continued growth towards becoming a competent Data Engineer.
