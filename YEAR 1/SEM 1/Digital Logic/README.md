# ⚡ SECR1013 Digital Logic

## 📚 Overview

Digital Logic introduces the fundamental concepts of digital systems and computer hardware. The course covers number systems, Boolean algebra, logic gates, combinational circuits, and sequential circuits which form the basis of modern computer architecture.

## 🌱 Reflection

Digital Logic was one of the most content-intensive courses during the semester. Learning topics such as Boolean algebra, logic gates, combinational circuits, and sequential circuits provided valuable insight into how computers operate at the hardware level. Understanding how simple gates can be combined to build complex digital systems was particularly interesting and helped strengthen logical reasoning skills.
