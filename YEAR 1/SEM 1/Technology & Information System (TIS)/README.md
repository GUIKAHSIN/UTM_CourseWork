# 🌐 SECP1513 Technology & Information System

## 📚 Overview

Technology & Information System provides an introduction to information technology, computer systems, networking, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies. The course also includes industry exposure activities to broaden understanding of the technology sector.

## 📝 Evidence

### Industry Talk & Industry Visit

Participation in industry talks and visits that provided exposure to real-world technology environments and career opportunities.

### PC Assemble

Practical activity involving computer hardware assembly and identification of system components.

## 🌱 Reflection

Technology & Information System provided valuable exposure to different areas within the technology industry. Activities such as industry talks, industry visits, and PC assembly sessions offered practical learning experiences beyond classroom theory. The course broadened understanding of how technology supports organizations and introduced concepts that later became relevant in software development, analytics, and data engineering studies.
