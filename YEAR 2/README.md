# 📝 Reflection

Year 2 expanded upon the computing foundations established in Year 1 by introducing more advanced topics in programming, databases, software engineering, networking, and data analysis. The courses required a deeper understanding of problem-solving techniques and exposed me to different aspects of software and system development.

Programming-related subjects such as Data Structure & Algorithm, Object-Oriented Programming, and Data Analytics Programming strengthened coding skills and introduced more structured approaches to software development. At the same time, Database and Software Engineering courses provided valuable insight into how real-world systems are designed, documented, and managed.

Several assignments and projects throughout the year also introduced concepts related to data management, system development, and analytical thinking, which further increased my interest in the Data Engineering field. These experiences helped develop both technical knowledge and practical skills that would become important in more advanced courses during Year 3.

Overall, Year 2 served as a bridge between the fundamental concepts learned in Year 1 and the specialized Data Engineering, analytics, and software development topics explored in later semesters.
